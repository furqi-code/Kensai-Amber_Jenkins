Uniform Inspection Checklist

Service Dress Blues:
[ ] Cover — no stains, proper shape
[ ] Collar devices — centered, no tarnish
[ ] Ribbons — correct order, no bent pins
[ ] Rate — CPO anchor, centered
[ ] Name tag — correct spelling
[ ] Shoes — spit-shined, no scuffs
[ ] Trousers — proper break, no fraying

NWU Type III:
[ ] Tapes — IR, no fraying
[ ] Patches — command, US Navy, rank
[ ] Boots — polished, serviceable soles
[ ] Belt — no fraying, keeper aligned
