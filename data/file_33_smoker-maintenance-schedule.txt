Smoker Maintenance Schedule 2026

MONTHLY
- Empty ash pan and grease trap
- Check door gasket seal
- Wire brush cooking grates

QUARTERLY
- Deep clean interior with degreaser
- Check/probe thermometer calibration
- Grease auger motor on pellet smoker
- Check fire pot for clinkers

ANNUALLY (January)
- Repaint exterior if needed
- Replace all gaskets
- Check welds on offset smoker
- New thermometer probes
