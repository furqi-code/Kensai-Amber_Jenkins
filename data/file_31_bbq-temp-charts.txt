BBQ Temperature Reference

Beef Brisket: 200-205F internal
Pork Butt: 197-203F
Pork Ribs (spare): 195-200F
Pork Ribs (baby back): 185-190F
Chicken thighs: 170-175F
Turkey: 160F breast, 175F thigh
Fish: 145F

Smoker temp: 225-275F depending on meat
Thicker smoke at 225, faster cook at 275.
Wrap: Peach paper at 165F for brisket. Foil at 160F for pork.
