Amber's Rub Recipes

PORK RUB
8 tbsp brown sugar
4 tbsp paprika
2 tbsp kosher salt
2 tbsp black pepper
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tbsp onion powder
1 tsp cayenne

BEEF RUB
4 tbsp coarse black pepper
4 tbsp kosher salt
2 tbsp granulated garlic

CHICKEN RUB:
Same as pork but no sugar, add 2 tbsp cumin.

FISH RUB:
2 tbsp lemon pepper
1 tbsp dill
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
