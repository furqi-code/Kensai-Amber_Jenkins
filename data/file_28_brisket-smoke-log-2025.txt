Brisket Smoke Log — June 2025

Meat: 14lb prime packer
Rub: Salt + pepper + garlic 2:2:1
Smoker: Lang 60, oak splits
Cook temp: 250-275F
Time: 14 hours total
Wrap: Peach paper at 170F internal
Rest: 2 hours in cooler
Result: Good bark, tender flat, decent smoke ring. 3rd place at Salem County BBQ.
Lessons: Wrap earlier next time. Spritz every hour.
